League of Legends is free to play MOBA style video game for the PC & MAC. It is one of the most played video game in the world! Boasting more than 67 Million people playing Worldwide! The most played game mode consists of a player choosing a Champion and teaming up with 4 other real players or computer controlled Champions and matching up against another 5 man team and working together to take down the other teams base or 'Nexus'

Champions fall within 6 major roles:

Assassin
Fighter
Mage

Support
Tank
Marksman

    Assassin's have the following features

  1. High mobility
  2. High burst damage
  3. Low base Armor
  4. Weak early in laning phase

    Fighter's have the following features

  1. High continuous damage
  2. Survive well in longer team fights
  3. Short attack range
  4. Can be harrased easily by ranged Champions

    Mage's have the following features

  1. Long range
  2. High magic damage
  3. Dependent on mana for attacks
  4. Have to wait for ability cooldown's

    Support's have the following features

  1. Can heal teammates
  2. Enhance damage output from teammates
  3. Dependent on lane partner to get items
  4. Can have low health and/or low damage output

    Tanks's have the following features

  1. Take damage from enemy
  2. Good at initial engage onto enemy team
  3. Low mobility
  4. Low attack damage output

    Marksman's have the following features

  1. High damage output
  2. Long range attacks
  3. low defense against magic
  4. Can be item dependent for damage

How to play the game

The basic game is a 5 on 5 team game where you select a Champion and go onto a map called "Summoner's Rift". You are paired with 4 other teamates that can be either real people or computer controlled depending on your selection. You then start the game where you control your character by moving your mouse and right clicking on the area of the map you want to go. If you want to attack you move your mouse over you target and again click the right button. If you want to use an ability you first press the key for the ability you want to use (either Q,W,E or R) and then left click on the target. You win the game by killing the opposing Champions and destroying "Towers" that are placed in "Lanes" on the map ultimately getting to the tower in the other teams base called the "Nexus" and destroying it.
